Shipping & Return Policy

At MeanBuy, we believe that our service continues beyond delivery, and perpetuates until you have total satisfaction after your purchase. Hence, we ensure the safe and on-time delivery of the product. We have partnered with the most reliable 3rd party courier service providers who excel in time management and quality provided by their services.

Our Shipping Policy includes the following:

  1. Shipping of orders across India is carried out by the most reliable shipping partners, with complete online tracking of all packages.
  2. We strive to ship most of our products at no cost to you. However, there may be a few products that require a minimal shipping cost.
  3. Products being purchased will be delivered to you as per the shipping schedule is given to you while placing the order. Our unique 'Wait & Save' will give you a quick reference on when the product is expected to be delivered to you.
  4. We’ll do our best to deliver your products to you on schedule and will ensure that you are updated via SMS and Email about the dispatch, transit status, and delivery of the products.
  5. There may be cases wherein the shipping may be delayed by 2 to 6 business days, depending on various factors including, but not limited to, transport, weather conditions, and possible custom clearances required for selected deliveries. Customers will be intimated about any such delays.
  6. Customers will be held liable for any misinterpretation of billing or delivery address. All shipping costs of non-delivery of products due to wrong delivery will be borne by the customer.
  7. Returns Shipping charges for a refund are applicable to Seller shipping charges
  8. Return Charges are applicable according to consumer Pincode which are real-time shipping fees given by our 3rd party shipping provider.
  9. Any damage to the product needs to be claimed within 24 hours after receiving the product.
  10. Any product that is missing in the order needs to be provided with the unboxing video of the order. We highly take care of the products and the consumer's request before packing the order.
  11. Due to the Pandemic Situation Of COVID-19, the delivery time may sometimes delay 5 to 7 days to maintain more hygiene and your safety.
  12. The maximum time for delivery is 24 days, Minimum delivery is 15 days for shipments.
  13. In case you find any issue with the delivered product, please send us an email with the invoice number at orderreturn@meanbuy.com

Cancellation before shipment:

  • Once we receive the refused product and verify its packaging condition, your refund will be processed within 24-48 business hours to your bank account/credit card account.

  • If you wish to cancel an order that has been shipped but has not yet been delivered, please get in touch with our Customer Support team on WhatsApp at @+91 7093427583

  • If you have received an item in a damaged/defective condition, or have been sent the wrong product, you can follow a few simple steps to initiate your return/refund within 5 days of receiving the order: Step 1: Contact our customer support team via email ((support@meanbuy.com)) within 5 business days of receiving the order.
  • Step 2: Provide us with your order ID details and your request to return/replace/refund the defective/wrong items in your order. Kindly share an image of the product and the invoice for our reference.
  • Step 3: We will pick up the products within 2-4 business days. The refund or replacement will be initiated only if the products are received by us in their original packaging with their seals, labels, and barcodes intact.
